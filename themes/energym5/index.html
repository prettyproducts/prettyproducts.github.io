01

Research and Choose

Explore the available electric car models and determine which one suits your needs, budget, and preferences. Pay attention to factors such as lease duration, mileage limits, and any additional charges for exceeding those limits.

02

Check for Incentives

Research government incentives, tax credits, and rebates that may be available for leasing an electric car. These incentives can significantly reduce the overall cost. Review the terms of the lease agreement carefully.

03

Visit Dealerships

Visit dealerships that offer electric cars and schedule test drives to experience the vehicle's performance firsthand. Ask questions about the leasing process and terms. Work with the dealer to determine the monthly lease payments based on the agreed-upon terms.
