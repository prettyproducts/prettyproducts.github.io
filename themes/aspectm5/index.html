TYPES OF CONTRACTS

  • Stock Purchase Agreement
  • Partnership Agreement
  • Operating Agreement
  • Loan Agreement

This contract outlines the terms and conditions for purchasing shares of a company's stock. It specifies the purchase price, the number of shares, and any warranties or representations made by the seller.

Investors in partnerships, such as limited partnerships (LPs) or limited liability partnerships (LLPs), enter into partnership agreements. These contracts define the roles, responsibilities, and profit-sharing arrangements among partners.

Investors in limited liability companies (LLCs) typically sign operating agreements. These agreements set forth the management structure, voting rights, and financial obligations of LLC members.

Investors may enter into loan agreements when providing loans to individuals or businesses. These contracts specify the loan amount, interest rate, repayment terms, and any collateral required.
