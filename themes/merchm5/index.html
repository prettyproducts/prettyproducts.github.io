FAQs about digital products

These FAQs should help provide a basic understanding of digital products and how to interact with them.

How do I purchase digital products?

You can purchase digital products from online stores, marketplaces, or directly from the product's website. Payment methods usually include credit/debit cards, digital wallets, or online payment platforms.

Are digital products different from physical products?

Yes, digital products are intangible and exist in electronic form, while physical products are tangible and can be held or touched.

Can I share digital products with others?

It depends on the product and the terms of the license. Some digital products allow sharing, while others have restrictions to prevent unauthorized distribution.x

What is DRM, and how does it relate to digital products?

DRM stands for Digital Rights Management, and it is a technology used to protect digital content from unauthorized copying and sharing. It's often used with digital products like e-books, music, and video games.

Do I own digital products I purchase?

In most cases, you purchase a license to use digital products, which may come with certain restrictions. You typically do not own the product itself but have the right to use it as specified in the license agreement.
