Get Started

During the marathon you can:

  • Learn to live in a state of flow and play
    You will feel inner harmony and confidence. Learn easily in a feminine way to embody their desires.
  • See new ways of development and self-realization
    Lots of insights await you. You realize that you don't have to go to a job you hate to live the life of your dreams.
  • Learn the psychology of abundance and success
    Eliminate your internal installations blocking success. Learn to think big and allow yourself the best.
  • Identify your true desires
    Remember what you wanted as a child. Understand what you really want now/. 
  • Give them a clear direction
    Correctly formulate each of your desires. Create your perfect wishlist that works.
  • You will remove obstacles that pretend your performance
    Get rid of the accumulated negativity and imposed stereotypes. Work through deep-seated fears and false beliefs.
